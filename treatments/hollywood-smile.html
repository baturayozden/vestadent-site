What it is

Understanding hollywood smile.

A 'Hollywood Smile' is a complete cosmetic redesign of the teeth that show when you smile. Rather than a single procedure, it's a tailored combination of treatments — which may include veneers, crowns, bonding, whitening or implants — planned together for a harmonious result.

At Vestadent we design the smile digitally first, considering your facial proportions, lip line and personality, so the outcome looks natural and suits you rather than following a one-size-fits-all template.

Who it's for

You may be suitable if

  • ✓You'd like a comprehensive improvement to your smile
  • ✓You have several concerns — colour, shape, alignment or gaps
  • ✓You want a result designed around your face, not a template
  • ✓Your gums and remaining teeth are healthy (we'll assess)
  • ✓You want to preview the design before committing

We'll also consider

  • !A makeover may combine several treatments over more than one visit
  • !Some treatments (e.g. veneers) involve irreversible preparation
  • !We prioritise dental health first, aesthetics second
  • !Honest planning means we'll advise against anything unnecessary
  • !Results are designed to be natural rather than artificially uniform

Only an in-person assessment can confirm what's right for you — that's what your consultation is for.

FAQ

Common questions.

What does a Hollywood Smile involve?

It's a tailored plan rather than one procedure. Depending on your needs it may combine veneers, crowns, bonding, whitening or implants — all designed together for a balanced result.

Will my smile look fake?

Not with careful design. We plan around your facial features and natural tooth translucency, and you preview and approve the look before treatment so it suits you.

How much does a Hollywood Smile cost?

Because every makeover is bespoke, the cost depends on the treatments involved. We provide a detailed written plan and quote after your consultation.

Is it permanent?

Some elements are long-lasting but may eventually need maintenance or replacement. We'll explain the expected lifespan of each part of your plan.

Will it damage my natural teeth?

We always favour the most conservative option that achieves your goal, and we'll explain anything irreversible before you decide.
