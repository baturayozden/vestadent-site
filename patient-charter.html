Patient Charter

Last updated: 26 June 2026

This charter sets out what you can expect from the team at Vestadent, and what we ask of you in return, so that we can look after your dental health well and treat you with respect.

What you can expect from us

  • Respect and dignity. We will treat you courteously and without discrimination, and listen to your concerns.
  • Clear information. We will explain your diagnosis, treatment options, risks and benefits in plain language so you can make informed decisions.
  • Your consent. We will not begin treatment without your agreement, and you may ask questions or change your mind at any point.
  • Transparent fees. We will give you a written treatment plan and estimate before treatment begins, so you know what to expect.
  • Quality care. Your care will be provided by clinicians registered with the General Dental Council, working to current professional standards in a practice regulated by the Care Quality Commission.
  • Confidentiality. We will keep your information secure and private, in line with our Privacy Policy.
  • A clean, safe environment. We follow recognised infection-prevention and safety procedures.
  • Access and timeliness. We will try to see you promptly, run to time, and explain any delays.
  • Emergencies. We will give you advice on how to obtain urgent dental care when the practice is closed. [TODO: add the practice's out-of-hours / emergency arrangements.]

What we ask of you

  • Give us accurate information about your health and medical history, and tell us about any changes.
  • Arrive on time for appointments, and give us at least 2 working days' notice if you need to cancel or rearrange. Late cancellations and missed appointments may be subject to a fee, and a deposit may be requested for implant or longer appointments.
  • Treat our team and other patients with courtesy and respect.
  • Follow the care and aftercare advice we give you, and ask if anything is unclear.
  • Let us know promptly if you have any concerns, so we can help.

Your feedback

We welcome your comments — they help us improve. If something has not gone well, please see our Complaints Procedure. You can also share feedback at any time by emailing info@vestadent.co.uk or calling 0203 302 9290.
