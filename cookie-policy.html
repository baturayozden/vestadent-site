Cookie Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026

This policy explains how this website (vestadent.co.uk) uses cookies and similar technologies. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, to improve your experience, and to give site owners information about how their site is used. Similar technologies (such as fonts and maps loaded from other services) can also store or read information on your device.

2. How we use cookies

We aim to keep the use of cookies on this site to a minimum. Our website is a single, informational page and does not require you to log in or create an account.

The cookies and third-party resources used on this site fall into the following categories:

Strictly necessary

These are needed for the website to display and function correctly and cannot be switched off. They do not store information that identifies you for marketing.

Third-party content

To deliver the design and features of this site, we load some resources from third parties. When your browser requests these, the third party may receive your IP address and set its own cookies, governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google Fonts & Google Maps — used for typography and the location map. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Content delivery networks (cdnjs / Cloudflare) — used to load the animation libraries that power the page.

Analytics and marketing

This site uses only strictly necessary cookies and does not currently use analytics or advertising cookies. If we introduce analytics or marketing cookies in future, we will update this policy and add a cookie consent banner so you can accept or reject non-essential cookies before they are set.

3. Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers let you block or remove cookies, and to be warned before they are stored. Please note that blocking some cookies may affect how parts of the site work. Guidance for common browsers is available at aboutcookies.org.

4. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the website develops. The date at the top shows when it was last revised.
