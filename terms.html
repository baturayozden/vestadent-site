Private dental treatments including dental implants, All-on-4 and All-on-6, cosmetic and restorative dentistry (veneers, crowns, composite bonding), teeth whitening, oral surgery and related consultations. Book by phone, email, WhatsApp or the enquiry form on this website. An appointment is confirmed once we have acknowledged it. Some appointments (e.g. longer treatment or laboratory-based work) may require a deposit to secure the booking; any deposit is applied toward the cost of your treatment. Please arrive on time; late arrival may mean the appointment is shortened or rebooked.

We do not charge a fee for cancellations or missed appointments. As a courtesy to other patients, please give as much notice as you can if you need to change or cancel — at least 2 working days where possible. 5. Pricing & charging

We are a private practice; all treatment is paid for privately (no NHS charges). We will not start chargeable treatment without your agreement to the plan and cost. Fees are as quoted in your written estimate; if your treatment needs change we will discuss and agree any change in cost before proceeding. Payment is due as set out in your treatment plan, unless a finance arrangement is in place.

  • Treatment not yet started: where you have paid in advance for treatment that has not started and no costs have been incurred, we will refund the amount paid.
