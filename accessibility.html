Accessibility

Last updated: 26 June 2026

We want as many people as possible to be able to use the Vestadent website. This statement explains how accessible the site is, the steps we have taken, and how to contact us if you experience a problem.

Our aim

We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at level AA as our target standard, and to keep improving over time.

Steps we have taken

  • Text is set in legible fonts with the intention of maintaining readable colour contrast.
  • The site is built to work across modern browsers and to adapt to mobile, tablet and desktop screens.
  • Images that convey information include descriptive alternative text.
  • Animations and motion effects are reduced automatically if your device or browser is set to "reduce motion".

Known limitations

We are aware that parts of the site may not yet be fully accessible, and we are working to address them. These may include:

  • interactive features such as the 3D model viewers and drag controls, which rely on pointer interaction;
  • the custom cursor effect on pointer-based devices;
  • third-party content such as the embedded map.

We keep these limitations under review and work to improve them. If you have difficulty using any part of the site, please contact us using the details below and we will help — including providing the information in an alternative format wherever we can.

Getting help

If you need information from this site in a different format, or you experience any barrier using it, please contact us and we will do our best to help:
